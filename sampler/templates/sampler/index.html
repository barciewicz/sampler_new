Search for a YouTube video, then drag it to a sampler to slice it.
Download the slices for further refinement in your hardware/software sampler of choice.

Keep in mind that YouTube limits the daily quota of search requests that can be performed from a particular app,
so if you need to perform a lot of them, just browse through YouTube itself and use the "Load custom URL" sampler feature here.

Sampler controls:

  • In the waveform hold left mouse button to draw / move a sample. Right click to erase it.
  • In the pads click the left / right mouse button to play / remove a sample. Drag to swap samples.
